Directorate of Research

SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST) have taken various research initiatives over the years to mobilize the available knowledge resources for research and also, to come up strongly with innovative solutions.

RESEARCH WINGS

IIISM

To promote the research in the field of traditional system of Indian Medicine, including Siddha, Ayurveda and Unani, IIISM was established in 2008.

Research Institute

As a special research initiative of SRMIST, SRM Research Institute (SRMRI) came into existence in January 2012 to realize a great vision of the President

CENR

Center for Environmental Nuclear Research (CENR), was established in 2009 at SRMIST with the aid of special grants from BRNS, Mumbai under the Department of Atomic Energy (DAE).

Nanotechnology Research

The Nanotechnology Research Center is established with the aim of excelling to create the state-of-the-art facilities for nanotechnology research, development and products.

Earthquake Research Cell

Advanced Research on the causes for origin, occurrence and prediction of Earthquake by better understanding the geodynamical behavior.

SRM-DBT Platform

SRM-DBT platform for advanced life science technologies holds core facilities in Genomics, Proteomics and Bio-imaging.

Medical Research Centre

An exclusive Medical Research Centre was established in 2008, with the avowed objective of promoting research in medical and allied sciences.

Centre for Statistics

Centre for Statistics is the hub of Statistical Training, Research and Consultancy within the SRM Institute of Science & Technology. .

Vision:

SRMIST aims to emerge as a leading world-class university that creates and disseminates knowledge upholding the highest standards of instructions in Medicine & Health Sciences, Engineering & Technology, Management, Law, Science and Humanities.

Mission:

*To create a diverse community campus that inspires freedom and innovation.
*Promote excellence in educational and skill development processes.
*Continue to build productive international alliances.
